Installing GEANT4 on Linux

The GEANT4 toolkit is the most widely used particle simulation toolkit, with applications in fields ranging from medical science to space science. It is used worldwide for both commercial and academic purposes. The toolkit, created by the international GEANT4 collaboration, is written in C++, implements Monte Carlo Simulations, and utilizes object-oriented programming. This guide will walk you through the step by step proccess to install it from source.
